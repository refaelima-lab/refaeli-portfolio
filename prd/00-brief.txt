Portfolio Experience Brief / Site Structure PRD


PORTFOLIO EXPERIENCE BRIEF
Project Overview
A one-page portfolio experience for Principal Product Designer / Experience Architect Refaeli Ma.
The site should feel:
* intelligent
* restrained
* architectural
* cinematic
* highly intentional
* minimal but deep
The portfolio is not intended to function as a traditional UX case study repository.
It should feel like an authored narrative experience that reveals:
* systems thinking
* design philosophy
* organizational impact
* behavioral design intelligence
* cross-disciplinary range
The experience should prioritize:
* storytelling clarity
* pacing
* emotional rhythm
* elegant interaction
* progressive disclosure
The overall impression should feel closer to:
* an Apple internal strategy presentation
* a design manifesto
* a cinematic systems document
rather than:
* a startup portfolio template
* a Dribbble showcase
* an overly animated agency site
EXPERIENCE PRINCIPLES
1. Motion Should Support Understanding
Micro interactions and transitions should:
* guide attention
* reinforce hierarchy
* create pacing
* support immersion
* reveal relationships between elements
Motion should feel:
* subtle
* smooth
* atmospheric
* restrained
* confident
2. Negative Space Is Structural
Whitespace is not empty space.
It is part of the storytelling system.
The layout should breathe heavily:
* large margins
* intentional pacing
* controlled density
* asymmetrical composition when appropriate
Content should feel curated, not compressed.
3. Progressive Disclosure
The portfolio should not reveal everything immediately.
The experience should unfold gradually:
* simple → complex
* philosophy → evidence
* abstract → concrete
* systems → execution
The viewer should continuously discover deeper layers while scrolling.
4. Systems Over Screens
The portfolio should emphasize:
* relationships
* structures
* thinking
* ecosystems
* operating models
not just polished interfaces.
UI should appear as evidence of systems thinking, not as decoration.


OVERALL EMOTIONAL ARC
The homepage should progressively transition through:
1. Curiosity
2. Perspective
3. Credibility
4. Methodology
5. Proof
6. Creative depth
7. Human connection
