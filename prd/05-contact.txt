Interaction Requirements
Form Interaction
The form should feel:
* clean
* lightweight
* premium
* easy to complete
States required:
* regular
* focus
* hover
* error
* success
Focus states should be clear but subtle.
Avoid:
* heavy outlines
* generic browser defaults
* overly bright validation colors
Success State
After submission, show a warm confirmation message.
Suggested copy:
Message sent. I’ll get back to you soon.
Error State
Show concise, human error messaging.
Suggested copy:
Something didn’t go through. Please check your email or try again.

Visual Direction

Visual tone:
* spacious
* minimal
* warm
* refined
* slightly more personal than previous sections
Potential design elements:

* low-contrast background shift
* soft divider from previous section
* quiet hover animations on links and button
Avoid:
* loud footer-style CTA block
* aggressive sales language
* excessive form styling
________________

The viewer should feel:
“This is someone thoughtful, approachable, and worth reaching out to.”

Contact Section Add-on: Ask AI
Feature Name
ASK AI
Purpose
Add an interactive AI-powered experience inside the Contact section that allows viewers to ask questions about Refaeli’s work, experience, portfolio, design philosophy, and case studies.
This feature should feel like:
* a smart portfolio concierge
* a guided conversation layer
* a more interactive way to understand the designer
* a bridge between passive viewing and direct contact
It should not replace the contact form.
It should support exploration before reaching out.
________________


Reference Prototype
Claude has already created a demo in:
reference/prototype_sticky_askai
Use this prototype as the interaction and behavior reference.
Do not copy it blindly. Refine it to match the final portfolio visual system:
* sleek
* minimal
* premium
* calm
* intelligent
* consistent with the site’s motion and typography
________________


Placement
Place Ask AI within the Contact section, alongside or near the contact form.
Recommended layout:
* Left side: Contact copy, social links, résumé
* Right side: Contact form
* Ask AI: sticky floating assistant or integrated panel within the Contact area
Alternative:
* Add a small sticky Ask AI entry point near the lower-right corner once users reach the Contact section
* Expand into a compact chat panel when clicked
________________


Content / Entry Copy
Label
Ask AI
Supporting Copy
Curious about my work? Ask the AI version of my portfolio.
Alternative:
Ask about my projects, process, experience, or how I think through design problems.
Keep it short and warm.
________________


Suggested Prompt Starters
Include 4 to 6 suggested questions to help viewers begin.
Recommended prompts:
* What kind of designer is Refaeli?
* What is her strongest case study?
* How does she approach complex systems?
* What makes her different from a typical product designer?
* What did she do on Verizon Home App?
* Is she more strategy or execution?
Tone should feel direct and useful, not gimmicky.
________________


Interaction Requirements
Default State
Ask AI should appear as:
* compact
* inviting
* non-intrusive
Potential formats:
* small floating button
* compact card
* sticky mini-panel
* subtle assistant orb
Avoid:
* chatbot cliché
* loud AI branding
* oversized widget
* distracting animation
________________


Expanded State
When opened, Ask AI should reveal:
* short intro message
* input field
* suggested prompts
* response area
* close/minimize control
The panel should feel:
* polished
* lightweight
* integrated into the portfolio
* not like a third-party widget
________________


Visual Direction
Ask AI should use the same visual language as the site:
* glass effect
* subtle glow
* soft motion
* clean typography
* minimal icons
* calm contrast
It can echo the hero animation language:
* dots
* lines
* small orbiting motion
* subtle intelligence-system feel
Avoid:
* robot icons
* neon gradients
* overly literal AI visuals
________________


Behavior Requirements
Sticky Behavior
Since the reference is prototype_sticky_askai, preserve the sticky behavior.
Requirements:
* Ask AI should remain accessible while users are in or near the Contact section
* It should not cover important content
* It should collapse gracefully on scroll
* It should remain usable on mobile
Mobile Behavior
On mobile:
* render as a compact button
* expand into a full-width bottom sheet or modal
* keep input easy to reach
* avoid covering the contact form permanently
________________


AI Response Scope
The AI should answer based only on portfolio-approved content.
It can discuss:
* Refaeli’s experience
* case studies
* design philosophy
* principles
* skills
* contact information
* résumé summary
* project summaries
It should avoid:
* making claims not supported by the portfolio
* exposing confidential information
* inventing metrics
* speaking as Refaeli in first person unless clearly styled that way
* pretending to know private details not included in the site
Recommended system behavior:
Answer questions using only the portfolio content provided. If something is not covered, say that the portfolio does not include that detail and suggest contacting Refaeli directly.
________________


Personality / Tone
The assistant should sound:
* helpful
* concise
* thoughtful
* slightly warm
* aligned with Refaeli’s portfolio voice
It should not sound:
* overly salesy
* robotic
* generic
* exaggerated
________________


Example Opening Message
Hi, I’m trained on this portfolio. Ask me about Refaeli’s work, design philosophy, case studies, or experience.
Alternative with more personality:
Ask me anything about Refaeli’s work, from systems design to the problem beneath the problem.
________________


CTA Bridge
After a few questions, subtly encourage direct contact.
Example:
Want to talk through a role, project, or collaboration? Send Refaeli a note below.
This should not feel pushy.
________________


Success Criteria
Ask AI is successful if it:
* helps visitors understand the portfolio faster
* makes the Contact section more memorable
* reinforces Refaeli’s AI-forward positioning
* increases confidence before reaching out
* feels like part of the site, not an added plugin
________________


Emotional Goal
The viewer should feel:
“Even the contact section has a point of view. This designer thinks about interaction, intelligence, and storytelling all the way through.”
