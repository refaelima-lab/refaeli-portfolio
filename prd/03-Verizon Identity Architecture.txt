Homepage Content
Title
VERIZON IDENTITY ARCHITECTURE
________________


Body Copy
Leading the evolution of Verizon’s Identity & Profile ecosystem, designing the invisible architecture that connects households, devices, behaviors, and services into one intelligent system.
Built to power personalization, behavioral intelligence, and AI-driven experiences across Home and Mobile, this work shifts Verizon from managing products to understanding relationships, transforming a traditional utility platform into a connected operating system for modern households.
This work spans Unified Parental Controls, Authentication, Profile Architecture, Device Attribution, Personalization Systems, and AI-powered synthetic user frameworks, shaping the infrastructure future Verizon experiences will be built upon.
