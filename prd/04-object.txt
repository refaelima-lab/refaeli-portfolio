Objects / Physical Works Section Specification
Purpose
This section reveals the broader creative dimension behind the digital work.
It exists to show:
* material thinking
* industrial design background
* visual experimentation
* physical form exploration
* emotional and aesthetic sensibility
This section should feel less structured than the digital product work.
The emphasis is:
* atmosphere
* craft
* form
* mood
* physicality
This is not a technical case study section.
It should feel:
* cinematic
* tactile
* artistic
* immersive
* emotionally driven
The viewer should feel:
“This designer thinks beyond interfaces.”
________________

Minimal.

Section Title
OTHER THINGS I MAKE
Typography should feel:
* editorial

Avoid:

Supporting Copy
Not everything lives on a screen.
Some ideas take shape in materials, form, and experiments.
Tone:
* reflective
* calm
* understated
* human
________________


Background Direction
Keep the atmosphere minimal and cinematic.
Potential visual elements:
* geometric line systems
* subtle particle movement
* soft gradients
* slow ambient motion
* abstract structural forms
The visual system should feel connected to the rest of the portfolio while becoming:
* softer
* more tactile
* more emotional
Avoid:
* heavy UI aesthetics
* loud futuristic visuals
* chaotic animation
________________


Gallery Experience Requirements
Asset Location
assets/objects/
This section should function more like:
* a cinematic gallery
* a curated exhibition
* a moving moodboard
rather than:
* a standard portfolio grid
________________


Gallery Interaction Direction
The gallery should feel:
* immersive
* premium
* scroll-driven
* atmospheric
* highly visual
Potential interaction directions:
Sticky Scroll (Preferred)
As the user scrolls:
* one project locks/sticks in place
* imagery becomes immersive and large-scale
* the next project gradually transitions upward
* projects reveal sequentially
This creates:
* pacing
* focus
* cinematic immersion
The scroll should feel:
* deliberate
* smooth
* elegant
Avoid:
* abrupt snapping
* fast transitions
* carousel-like behavior
________________




Visual Composition Requirements
This section is heavily mood-driven.
The imagery should not simply appear on plain backgrounds.
Instead:
* integrate supporting atmospheric visuals
* create cinematic environments
* use lighting and depth
* create emotional tone around the work
Potential techniques:
* layered background imagery
* gradients
* spatial textures
* blurred environment references
* reflective lighting
* shadows and depth
The goal is:
make the work feel iconic and emotionally charged.
________________


Motion Requirements
Motion should feel:
* slow
* immersive
* premium
* gallery-like
Potential motion:
* image scaling
* depth transitions
* layered parallax
* atmospheric drifting
* fade transitions
* subtle motion blur
Avoid:
* aggressive interaction
* flashy animation
* rapid movement
* distracting effects
________________


Project Structure
Each project should contain:
* project title
* role
* category
* year
* optional award logos
* large cinematic visual treatment
The typography should remain minimal so the visuals dominate.
