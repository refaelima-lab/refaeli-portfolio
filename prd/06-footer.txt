Footer Specification
Purpose
The footer exists as a quiet closing moment.
There is no major functional or conversion goal for this section.
It should simply provide:
* closure
* personality
* a final human touch
The footer should feel:
* minimal
* calm
* lightly playful
* intentional

________________
Tone
The footer should maintain the portfolio’s overall sophistication while allowing a small moment of personality.
The humor should feel:
* subtle
* intelligent
* understated
* human
Avoid:
* meme energy
* loud jokes
* excessive animation
* overly casual styling
________________
Visual Direction
The footer should remain:
* visually lightweight
* spacious
* low contrast
* highly legible
Typography should stay readable even with reduced emphasis.
Potential direction:
* smaller typography scale
* soft opacity hierarchy
* generous spacing
* subtle divider from contact section
* minimalist layout structure
________________
Layout Requirements
Potential structure:
* left-aligned copyright
* centered playful line
* right-aligned location
Or stacked vertically on smaller screens.
Spacing should feel:
* breathable
* uncluttered
* intentional
________________
Interaction Requirements
Keep interactions minimal.
Optional:
* subtle hover response on playful line
* very soft opacity transition
Avoid:
* animated footer gimmicks
* scrolling marquees
* large motion effects
________________
Emotional Goal
The viewer should leave the site feeling:
“Thoughtful, intentional, deeply designed, but still human.”
